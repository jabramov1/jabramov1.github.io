Problem

Make change safe.

Transfer Billing needed reliable customer emails for state changes, reminders, and error conditions. The challenge was not sending one email; it was adding new email types without duplicating logic or creating inconsistent behavior. I also learned how much production reliability depends on small pieces like DLQs, early invalid-event routing, and idempotency.

Architecture

Small Lambda, clear owners.

  1. Producer

    Billing servicesPublish lifecycle events when a notification is due.

  2. Router

    EventBridgeFilter events, attach template IDs, and reject invalid messages early.

  3. Executor

    LambdaValidate the request, enforce idempotency, and call the email service.

  4. Delivery

    Email serviceRender templates, retry delivery, and send the customer notification.
